My first post

The team at weather.us along with our parent company, Kachelmannwetter, have released our brand new “in-house” model, The CONUS Swiss HD! The Swiss HD model is a 4 km, high-res model focused on short-term forecasting. It is updated 4 times daily and is run for a period of 24 hours. It is exclusively ours – you won’t find it anywhere else!.
